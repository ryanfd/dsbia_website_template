Pannini of the Day

We make fresh panninis everyday! Our panninis come with fresh meats, like turkey or chicken, veggies, like spinach, tomatoes and peppers, and of course, cheese! All toasted in our herbed foccacia bread and topped with Tanja's special spreads and sauces. This warm and melty pannini is a great meal that will leave you fueled and satisfied! The pannini of the day also comes with your choice of salad or chips.

Rollup of the Day

Come and try our freshly made rollups! Everyday we work with a variety of meats, veggies and sauces to create a delicious product. Dishes like the turkey and cranberry rollup are delicious and people always want more! We also offer vegetarian options. Get your veggie fix and try the fresh veggie rollup if the meat-based rollup isn't for you. Rollups are also served with your choice of salad and chips.

Get to know us!

Cafe Nelly has been proudly serving the Whalley area for over 20 years. In 2018, Margaret and her husband Patrick bought the shop together to carry out Margaret’s dream of owning her own cafe. Her love of coffee and passion for creating delicious foods came from years of volunteering and leading her church’s after-service kitchen. After living in downtown Vancouver, this inspired her to create a cute neighborhood cafe where people could come together and connect in a cozy atmosphere.

Margaret is fully barista trained and has been using fresh ingredients to deliver quality bistro sandwiches and sweet treats to customers for over three years. Whether it’s grabbing a coffee with friends or enjoying a delicious business lunch, Cafe Nelly is the hidden gem you’ve been waiting for!
